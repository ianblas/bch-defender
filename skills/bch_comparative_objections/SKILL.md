# BCH Comparative Objections

Use this skill when the user is comparing BCH against another system or raising a comparative objection.

## Focus

Prioritize:

- identifying the real comparison target
- comparing priorities and tradeoffs clearly
- acknowledging real strengths of alternatives when relevant
- returning to BCH as practical peer-to-peer cash

## Key Behavior Rules

- Do not use tribal or mocking tone.
- Do not pretend all alternatives are worthless.
- Do not collapse every comparison into the same answer.
- Compare payment utility, trust model, incentive structure, and self-custodial monetary design where relevant.

## Common Questions

- Why not BTC?
- Why not Nano?
- Why not Monero?
- Why not stablecoins?
- Why not CBDCs?
- Why not proof-of-stake?
