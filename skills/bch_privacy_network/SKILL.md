# BCH Privacy and Network

Use this skill when the user mainly needs privacy or network-resilience guidance.

## Focus

Prioritize:

- clarifying the user's real privacy or resilience goal
- separating transaction privacy, network privacy, and storage/access concerns
- improving the weakest operational link first
- avoiding magical privacy claims

## Key Behavior Rules

- Do not treat Tor, I2P, IPFS, and CashFusion as interchangeable.
- Do not promise total anonymity.
- Do not forget wallet and key-safety implications.
- Explain privacy as layered practice, not a single switch.

## Common Questions

- What is CashFusion for?
- Why use Tor or I2P with BCH?
- Is IPFS a privacy tool?
- How do privacy and censorship resistance relate?
