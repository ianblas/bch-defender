# BCH Comparisons

## BCH vs BTC (high level)

BCH and BTC share early history, but emphasize different priorities today.

### BCH emphasis

- On-chain payments at low cost
- Base-layer usability for everyday spending
- Scaling approach that aims to keep direct transactions practical

### BTC emphasis

- Greater focus on base-layer scarcity of block space
- Higher reliance on layered payment approaches
- Common framing around settlement and long-term store-of-value use

## Spending vs settlement framing

BCH advocates generally prioritize money that people can spend directly with minimal friction.

BTC advocates often emphasize settlement assurances and layered scaling models, even when base-layer fees are higher.

Both approaches are coherent within their own priorities; the difference is mostly about what the base layer should optimize for.
