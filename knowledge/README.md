# Knowledge

## Purpose

This directory expands BCH Defender's reusable knowledge layer.

The goal is to convert recurring themes from the LEP2P episode catalog into structured knowledge areas that can support:

- FAQs
- explanations
- prompts
- datasets
- evaluations
- objections handling

## Scope

This expansion is organized into reusable topic maps rather than episode-by-episode notes.

It is designed to help BCH Defender answer recurring BCH questions with more depth and better thematic coverage.

## Included Knowledge Maps

- `core-concepts.md`
- `network-scaling-and-infrastructure.md`
- `privacy-identity-and-self-custody.md`
- `adoption-economics-and-use-cases.md`
- `comparative-and-critical-topics.md`

## Design Principle

Knowledge documents should stay:

- BCH-first
- concept-driven
- reusable across support, objections, and education
- broad enough to capture recurring themes
- simple enough to inform prompts and datasets later
