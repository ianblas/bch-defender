# BCH Payments

## Payment characteristics

- **Fast to send and receive:** recipients can see incoming payments quickly.
- **Low-fee by design:** supports small and medium transactions without large percentage loss to fees.
- **Borderless:** works across countries without depending on card networks.
- **Permissionless:** users generally do not need prior approval to create a wallet and transact.

## Why this matters for users

- Better fit for remittances and international payments
- Useful for online commerce and direct transfers
- Reduced reliance on intermediaries that can delay or block payments

## Why this matters for merchants

- Low transaction costs
- No chargebacks at the protocol layer
- Ability to accept payments from global customers
- Simple integration through wallets, payment processors, or plugins
