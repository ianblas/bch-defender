# Merchant Adoption

## Why merchants may accept BCH

- **Ultra-low fees:** can improve margins, especially on small-ticket sales.
- **No chargebacks:** confirmed BCH transactions are final on-chain.
- **Global customer reach:** accept payments from users worldwide.
- **Direct settlement option:** receive funds without card-network intermediaries.

## Integration options

Merchants can start with:
- a simple wallet and QR checkout
- a BCH payment processor
- e-commerce plugins or API integrations

## Practical operating benefits

- Works for online and in-person payments
- Can settle in BCH or convert to fiat, depending on business preference
- Useful as an additional payment rail alongside cards and cash
