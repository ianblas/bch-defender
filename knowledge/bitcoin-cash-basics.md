# Bitcoin Cash Basics

## What is BCH?

Bitcoin Cash (BCH) is a cryptocurrency focused on peer-to-peer electronic cash: sending money directly between users with low fees and fast settlement visibility.

## Common use cases

- Everyday payments (online and in-person)
- Cross-border transfers
- Merchant acceptance
- Savings for users who prefer a fixed-supply digital asset

## Spending and saving

BCH can be used for both spending and savings:
- **Spending:** low fees and direct transfers make day-to-day payments practical.
- **Savings:** BCH has a fixed maximum supply of 21 million, which some users view as a sound-money property.

## Self-custody and control

Users can hold BCH in self-custody wallets where they control private keys and seed phrases. Custodial options also exist, but they require trusting a provider.

## Practical value proposition

BCH is often chosen for straightforward payment utility: send funds globally, quickly, and at low cost, while keeping the option to self-custody.
