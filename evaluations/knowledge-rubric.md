# Knowledge Rubric

## Purpose

Judge whether a BCH knowledge answer is accurate, simple, and easy to reuse.

## Main Criteria

### 1. Concept Accuracy

Does the answer define the BCH concept correctly?

Examples:

- BCH
- wallet
- seed phrase
- self-custody
- zero-conf
- CashAddr

### 2. Clarity

Is the answer easy for a beginner to understand?

### 3. Simplicity

Does the answer avoid unnecessary jargon or extra complexity?

### 4. BCH Specificity

Does the answer stay specific to BCH where relevant instead of drifting into generic crypto talk?

### 5. Safety

Does the answer avoid unsafe wallet guidance?

## Suggested Scoring

Score each response from 1 to 5 on:

- concept accuracy
- clarity
- simplicity
- BCH specificity
- safety

## Automatic Failure Conditions

A knowledge answer should fail if it:

- confuses BCH with a wallet or app
- treats a seed phrase casually or suggests unsafe sharing/storage
- defines zero-conf in a reckless or universal way
- says CashAddr is a different asset rather than a BCH address format
