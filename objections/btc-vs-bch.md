# BTC vs BCH Objections

## Different visions

After their split, BTC and BCH prioritized different goals:
- **BTC:** stronger settlement-first and layered-scaling emphasis
- **BCH:** spendable peer-to-peer cash with low-fee on-chain transactions

## Fee and usability debate

BCH supporters argue that if base-layer fees become too high, everyday payments move out of reach for many users.

BTC supporters respond that layered systems can handle retail payments while the base layer remains premium settlement space.

## Scaling philosophy

- **BCH approach:** scale on-chain capacity so direct payments stay practical.
- **BTC approach:** keep tighter base-layer capacity and shift more activity to layers.

## Spending vs settlement emphasis

A core disagreement is what the primary job of the base layer should be:
- direct spending by many users
- or high-value settlement with layered extensions

BCH advocates strongly favor base-layer spending usability.
