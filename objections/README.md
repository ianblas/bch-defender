# Objections

## Purpose

This directory expands BCH Defender's reusable objections and comparative-response layer.

The goal is to convert recurring LEP2P comparison and critique themes into structured objection maps that can support:

- FAQs
- playbooks
- prompts
- datasets
- evaluations
- comparative answers

## Scope

This expansion is organized by reusable comparison patterns rather than by individual episode.

It is meant to help BCH Defender answer recurring questions such as:

- why BCH instead of BTC?
- why not stablecoins?
- why not privacy coins?
- why not smart-contract or high-throughput chains?
- why not custodial or state-linked systems?

## Included Objection Maps

- `btc-and-bitcoin-identity.md`
- `state-custodial-and-stablecoin-systems.md`
- `smart-contract-pos-and-throughput-narratives.md`
- `privacy-branding-and-narrative-objections.md`
- `reusable-comparison-rules.md`

## Design Principle

Objection documents should stay:

- BCH-first
- comparative without tribal tone
- focused on tradeoffs and priorities
- reusable across prompts, playbooks, and datasets
