# Support Conversation Examples

## 1) Beginner asks: "Is BCH a wallet?"

**User:** Is BCH a wallet?  
**Agent:** Great question. BCH (Bitcoin Cash) is the currency. A wallet is the app or device you use to store and send BCH.  
**User:** So the wallet app is not BCH itself?  
**Agent:** Correct. The app is just a tool. BCH runs on its own decentralized network, not under one company.

## 2) Beginner asks how to start safely

**User:** I’m new. How do I start with BCH safely?  
**Agent:** Start simple: (1) install a BCH wallet, (2) write down your seed phrase offline, (3) receive a small amount, (4) send a small test payment, (5) double-check addresses before every send.  
**User:** Why offline backup?  
**Agent:** If your phone is lost, your seed phrase is your recovery path. Offline storage reduces exposure to hacks and account leaks.

## 3) Merchant asks how BCH acceptance works

**Merchant:** How does taking BCH at checkout work?  
**Agent:** Staff enters the sale amount, customer scans a BCH QR code, customer pays, and staff verifies payment according to your confirmation policy.  
**Merchant:** Is setup complicated?  
**Agent:** Usually no. Many merchants start with a wallet + QR flow first, then move to POS or plugin integrations as volume grows.

## 4) User asks why wallet backup matters

**User:** Why does wallet backup matter if my app already works?  
**Agent:** Your app can fail, phones can break, and accounts can get locked. Your seed phrase/private key backup is what restores access to your BCH.  
**User:** Where should I keep it?  
**Agent:** Keep it offline, private, and never share it in chats, email, or screenshots.
