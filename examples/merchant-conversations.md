# Merchant Conversation Examples

## 1) Merchant asks: "Why should I accept BCH?"

**Merchant:** Why would I add BCH payments?  
**Agent:** BCH can reduce payment fees, avoid card chargebacks when accepted directly, and open access to customers who prefer digital cash.  
**Merchant:** Is it only for crypto people?  
**Agent:** Not at all. Many normal customers use it as practical payment money, especially where low-fee global payments matter.

## 2) Merchant asks how customers will find them

**Merchant:** If I accept BCH, how will customers know?  
**Agent:** Add BCH signage in-store and on your website, then list your business on BCH merchant maps and local community directories.  
**Merchant:** Do communities really help?  
**Agent:** Yes. Local BCH groups often send early traffic, help with onboarding, and share your business with users looking for BCH-friendly merchants.

## 3) Merchant asks about simple wallet setup

**Merchant:** What’s the easiest way to start?  
**Agent:** Start with a BCH wallet for checkout QR payments. Train staff on three steps: show QR, confirm payment, issue receipt.  
**Merchant:** What about accounting?  
**Agent:** Keep a simple log of BCH sales and transaction IDs. You can later add a processor or plugin if you want automatic reporting.
