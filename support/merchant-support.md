# Merchant Support Guide

## Getting started with BCH acceptance

1. Choose a wallet, POS app, or payment processor.
2. Define checkout flow (QR, terminal, or online plugin).
3. Set confirmation policy by transaction size.
4. Set treasury policy: hold BCH, convert to fiat, or mixed.

## Payment flow basics

- Staff enters sale amount.
- Customer scans QR and pays.
- Staff verifies payment broadcast and confirmations per policy.
- Receipt is issued as with other payment methods.

## Staff training essentials

Train staff to:
- identify valid BCH payment completion
- handle address/QR errors calmly
- follow escalation steps for failed or delayed payments

## Refund workflow

- Request customer refund address explicitly.
- Verify address before sending.
- Record original payment and refund transaction IDs.
- Apply consistent refund policy and timing.

## Practical operations

- Post clear "BCH accepted" signage.
- Test payment flow before going live.
- Keep a quick internal checklist for shift staff.
- Reconcile BCH sales in accounting records regularly.
