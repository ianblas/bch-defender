# Recovery Phrase Safety

## Summary

A recovery phrase is the backup that gives you access to your BCH wallet.

If your phone is lost, the app is deleted, or the device breaks, the recovery phrase is what lets you restore control. If someone else gets the phrase, they may be able to take the funds. That is why recovery phrase safety is one of the most important BCH security practices.

## What a Recovery Phrase Is

A recovery phrase is a list of words, usually 12, 18, or 24, shown in a specific order.

Those words are not just a reminder. They are the backup to your wallet.

In practice, they are like the master key to your BCH.

## Why It Matters

Your recovery phrase matters because:

- it lets you restore your wallet
- it protects you from losing access if the device fails
- it gives you independence from any single phone or app
- it is the foundation of self-custody

Without the phrase, losing the device may mean losing the funds.

With the phrase, you can usually restore the wallet on another device.

## How to Write It Down Correctly

### 1. Write every word completely

Do not abbreviate.
Do not guess.
Do not invent your own shorthand.

### 2. Preserve the exact order

Order matters. The same words in the wrong order can produce the wrong wallet.

### 3. Double-check carefully

Review the phrase slowly before relying on it.

### 4. Keep it offline

Store it in a secure offline location whenever possible.

## Why Order Matters

The recovery phrase is not just a bag of words. The sequence is part of the backup.

If:

- one word is missing
- one word is misspelled
- one word is incomplete
- the order is wrong

then recovery may fail.

## Why It Must Never Be Shared

Anyone with your full recovery phrase may be able to control your BCH.

That is why you should never:

- send it by chat
- email it to someone
- share it with “support”
- post it online
- give it to a friend “just in case”

Treat it like the key to a safe full of money.

## How to Store It Safely

Good practice usually includes:

- keeping it offline
- storing it in a place only you control
- protecting it from casual discovery
- avoiding unnecessary duplication
- making sure you can still find it when needed

The goal is to protect it without making it impossible for you to recover your wallet later.

## Common Mistakes

- writing the words in the wrong order
- leaving one or more words incomplete
- taking a casual screenshot and forgetting about it
- storing the phrase where others can easily access it
- assuming you will remember it without a written backup
- giving the phrase to another person
- treating the phrase like a password reset code instead of full wallet control

## How to Explain It to Beginners

A simple way to explain it is:

**Your recovery phrase is the master backup to your BCH wallet. If you lose the device, the phrase can restore the funds. If someone else gets the phrase, they may be able to take them.**

## Short Answer

A recovery phrase should be written down completely, in the correct order, kept private, and stored safely offline. It is one of the most important parts of self-custody.

## Conclusion

Self-custody is powerful because it puts you in control. The recovery phrase is what makes that control durable.

Respect it, protect it, and never treat it casually.
