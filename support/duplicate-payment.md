# Duplicate Payment

## Summary

A customer may accidentally pay twice.

This should be treated as an operational BCH support case, not as a mystery.

The first job is to verify whether there are actually two valid BCH payments.

## Why It Matters

Under checkout pressure, staff may react too quickly.

That can lead to:

- refunding before verifying both payments
- refunding the wrong amount
- trusting screenshots instead of the actual transaction record

A clear process protects both the merchant and the customer.

## What to Verify

### 1. Confirm whether there are two separate BCH transactions

Use the merchant wallet, transaction IDs, or a BCH block explorer.

### 2. Confirm the amount of each payment

Do not assume both payments were for the same amount.

### 3. Confirm which payment the business intends to keep

The merchant should know whether the second payment is fully extra or whether part of it covered a difference.

## Step-by-Step Guidance

### 1. Verify before saying anything final

If there are not two real BCH transactions, it is not yet a duplicate payment case.

### 2. Identify the extra amount clearly

Separate the original sale amount from any excess paid.

### 3. Use the merchant refund process if needed

If there really was a duplicate BCH payment, handle the extra amount through a controlled refund process.

### 4. Record what was refunded

Keep track of:

- the original BCH transactions
- the refund amount
- the refund transaction

## Common Causes

- customer tapped Send twice
- customer thought the first payment did not go through
- merchant app or customer app looked delayed
- checkout pressure caused confusion

## Common Mistakes

- refunding based on a screenshot alone
- not confirming both BCH transactions exist
- sending a refund without documenting the amount
- treating a duplicate payment as if BCH itself malfunctioned

## Short Answer

If a customer seems to have paid twice, first confirm that two real BCH transactions exist. Then identify the extra amount and handle any refund in a controlled way.
